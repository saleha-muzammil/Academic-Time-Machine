# CONTEMPORARY ISSUES OF POLITICS

# 1. What is Digitalization?

Digitalization means:

> The increasing use of digital technologies (internet, social media, smartphones, AI, online platforms) in society, politics, economy, and communication.

It changes:

- how people communicate
- how governments work
- how political campaigns operate
- how information spreads

Politics has become digital.

---

# 2. Medialization / Digitalization

Meaning:  
Media no longer only reports events.

It now shapes:

- politics
- democracy
- culture
- religion
- youth behavior
- public opinion

# 5. Cultivation Theory

Developed by:

# George Gerbner

---

# Definition

Cultivation Theory says:

> Long-term media exposure shapes how people see reality.

People start believing:  
the world is like the media portrays it.

---

# Example

If media constantly shows:

- crime
- corruption
- violence

People may think:  
society is extremely dangerous even if statistics say otherwise.

---

# Political Connection

Media can shape:

- political opinions
- nationalism
- fear
- ideology
- public perception of leaders

---

# Important Quote

> “Media cultivates perceptions of reality.”

---

# 7. Confirmation Bias

Confirmation bias means:

> People prefer information that supports their existing beliefs.

---

# Example

If someone already supports a political party,  
they mostly follow:

- pages
- influencers
- friends

who support the same party.

---

# Result

Social media creates illusion:

> “Everybody agrees with me.”

---

# Political Effects

Confirmation bias can:

- strengthen extremism
- increase intolerance
- spread misinformation
- weaken critical thinking

---

# Important Exam Point

Social media does not always create informed citizens.

Sometimes it reinforces existing biases.

---

VERY IMPORTANT modern issue.

---

# Fake News

---

- viral content

---

# Political Effects

Fake news can:

- manipulate elections
- damage reputations

---

# Conspiracy Theories

Claims without strong evidence.

Examples:

- election conspiracies
- secret government plots
- misinformation campaigns
